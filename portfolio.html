AKFCU is a smallholder farmer-owned cooperative union based in the Eastern Hararghe Zone of Oromia regional State initially with working scope of five districts. Now the working scope of the union was affiliated in to ten districts namely; Kersa, Haramaya, Konbolcha, Jarso, Fedis, Gursum, Babile, Cineksan, Midhega-Tola and Mekenisa-oromo districts
