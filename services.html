    Coffee

    The Afre Kelo Farmers Cooperative Union specializes in providing high-quality coffee products. With a focus on promoting sustainable agriculture practices, the union brings together local farmers to cultivate and harvest coffee beans. By fostering a cooperative environment, Afre Kelo aims to enhance the overall quality of the coffee produced and ensure fair economic benefits for its members.

    Sorghum

    Afre Kelo Farmers Cooperative Union is also actively involved in sorghum cultivation and distribution. Recognizing the importance of diversifying agricultural activities, the union supports local farmers in cultivating sorghum, a versatile and resilient cereal grain.

    Potato

    Afre Kelo Farmers Cooperative Union focuses on coffee, sorghum, and potato cultivation. By uniting local farmers, the union promotes sustainable practices for high-quality produce.Additionally, Afre Kelo supports potato cultivation, emphasizing sustainability and economic benefits for farmers.

    Working Area

    Afran Kello Farmers’ Cooperative Union is a smallholder farmer-owned cooperative union based in the Eastern Hararghe Zone of Oromia regional State, Ethiopia.

    AKFCO

    The working scope of the union was 10 districts found in the Eastern Hararghe Zone namely; Kersa, Haramaya, Konbolcha, Jarso, Fedis, Gursum, Babile, Cineksan, Midhega-Tola and Mekenisa-oromo districts

    Headquarter

    The headquarter of the union was located at Maya City, Haramaya subcity, on the way of Haramaya University.

    Team

    The Union has nine key managements: General Manager, Deputy General Manager, Financial Department Head, Input and Supply Department Head, Plan and Evaluation Department Head, Human Resource Department Head, Farm Service Center Project Head, Seed Multplication Project Head, and Store Head, as well as a general assembly consisting of two representative chairperson and deputy chairperson from each of the one hundred and fifty-one cooperatives

    OUR VALUES

    Cooperatives are based on the values of self help, self-responsibility, democracy, equality, equity, and solidarity. In the tradition of their founders, cooperative members believe in the ethical values of honesty, openness, social responsibility and caring for others.
